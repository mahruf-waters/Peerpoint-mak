# **App Name**: PeerPoint

## Core Features:

- Personalized Academic Onboarding: Secure authentication and profile creation to track university targets, course interests, and specific subjects for institutions like Makerere and MUST.
- Entrance Exam Repository: A centralized archive for categorized past papers (Makerere, MUBS, etc.) with mobile-optimized viewing and localized filtering.
- Peer-to-Peer Academic Forum: Collaborative discussion space for students to post questions, share answers, and earn reputation points stored in Firestore.
- Interactive Revision Quizzes: Timed multiple-choice tests in Aptitude, English, and Mathematics with real-time scoring and leaderboards.
- AI Study Companion: An AI tool that provides detailed reasoning for complex past paper answers and identifies knowledge gaps from quiz performance.
- Candidate Dashboard: A high-performance landing page displaying exam countdowns, study streaks, and visual progress metrics across various universities.
- Cloud-Synced Progress Tracker: Persistent tracking of downloaded materials and quiz statistics across devices using a global state and centralized database.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Academic Blue (#2B59C3) chosen to evoke trust, authority, and professional growth.
- Background color: Mist White (#F1F3F8), a heavily desaturated variant of the primary hue providing a clean, focused scholarship atmosphere.
- Accent color: Vibrant Cerulean (#1797B3), providing high-contrast for primary call-to-actions and academic milestones.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif that brings a high-end, elegant, and vintage academic feel to section headers.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif chosen for its warmth and high legibility during long revision reading sessions.
- A structured, modular layout with high-impact cards, utilizing consistent rounding and whitespace for a breathable mobile-first experience.
- Soft micro-interactions for study streaks and page transitions to make the heavy task of exam preparation feel rewarding.
